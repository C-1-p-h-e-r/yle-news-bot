2025-05-20

Finnish government will cover the entire cost of providing flights between Helsinki and five regional airports. Minister of Transport and Communications Lulu Ranne said the decision was made in the interests of regional vitality. Kela: More fathers taking parental leave since 2022 law change

https://yle.fi/a/74-20162964

2025-05-20

Major parental leave reform that came into effect in August 2022 has significantly increased fathers’ use of parental benefits. Fathers of children born after the reform used nearly twice the share of parental allowance days compared to dads whose children were born before the change. The law changewas intended to allow parents more flexibility in how they divided up their parental leave, instead of setting defined periods for maternity and paternity leave. Under the reform, parental leave was in principle divided equally but a parent could transfer part of their quota to the other parent.
